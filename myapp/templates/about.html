Project Overview

Welcome to our Easy Hostel! Our project is designed to help students and visitors find hostels near a specific university area.

Whether you're a student looking for accommodation close to your campus or a visitor exploring the area, our Hostel Finder makes it easy to discover suitable hostel options.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the process of finding hostels near universities. We aim to provide a user-friendly platform that offers a comprehensive list of hostels, along with detailed information and user reviews.
